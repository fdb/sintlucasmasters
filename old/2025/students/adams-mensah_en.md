Is there life before birth, or does life begin after birth? Using this question as a starting point, the artist seeks answers through a layered soundscape.

Journey of the Intercountrian is an installation that offers visitors a brief escape from reality.

The installation unfolds across two spaces. The first space features the sounds of birth, while the second houses the Egg of Stories and the Fertility Calabash. Visitors can choose to lie inside the Egg to listen to the soundscape, or experience it through the Calabash.

The soundscape invites you on a journey to discover the story of the Intercountrian — a journey that may sound familiar, yet isn't.
