The first chapter as a "preview chapter" of Animal Farm through the eyes of Old Major.

This master project is a processing of the first chapter of George Orwell's Animal Farm, in the form of a publication. Old Major is a character who sees the beginning of the revolution on the farm in a dream, and is the most frequently appearing animal in this version.
